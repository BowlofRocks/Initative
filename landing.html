What is Venato?

Venato can track and save your order of initiative in tabletop games. You will be able to create your characters and monsters to track their initiatives in combat. You can save your character and monster in campaigns, so you can track your initiative in multiple runs.

We were inspired to create this to make combat run more smoothly and to help save time so get to the fun parts faster. It will help you focus on the important parts of the game instead of worrying who’s turn it is or which monster is up next.

Enchance your adventures today!
